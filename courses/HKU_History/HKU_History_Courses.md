## Notes & caveats

- **Source freshness.** All entries above are taken from the HKU History Department's *Courses Offered in 2025-2026* page (updated November 18, 2025) and the *Complete List of Courses offered by Other Departments*. The Department explicitly notes that the timetable is updated separately (last update January 13, 2026); always re-check there for semester times / room.
- **Not in current 2025-26 list.** A few HIST codes (HIST2193, HIST2233, HIST2179, HIST1025) circulated in older syllabi are not on the 2025-26 page. They may return in 2026-27 or live under different cross-listing; do not cite from this resource if you cannot find a current instructor.
- **Other-department courses** have no confirmed instructor for the 2025-26 cycle on the History Department page; confirm directly with the host department before enrolment.
- **Common Core instructor matching.** A few CC pages link to multiple staff URLs; the instructor shown is the one most likely to teach in 2025-26, but always check commoncore.hku.hk.
- **Tier rules of thumb.**
  - **T1** = course content directly supports a section / claim in the capstone.
  - **T2** = supplies a comparator, civilian voice, gender/race/law angle, or methodological option.
  - **T3** = the History Department's own research-training track, useful for the writing process rather than the content.
